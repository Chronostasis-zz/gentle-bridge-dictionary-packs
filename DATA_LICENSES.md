# Dictionary Data Licenses

Release manifests identify every upstream snapshot and license. Pack consumers must retain and display this provenance.

## Wiktionary / Kaikki

Kaikki data is extracted from Wiktionary using Wiktextract. Wiktionary-derived data is made available under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike license and GNU Free Documentation License, subject to the applicable Wiktionary edition terms.

- Kaikki: https://kaikki.org/dictionary/
- Wiktionary licensing: https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Policy:Terms_of_Use
- Wiktextract: https://github.com/tatuylonen/wiktextract

Generated packs preserve source IDs and attribution in their manifests. The pack-builder code's MIT license does not replace or narrow the data licenses.
